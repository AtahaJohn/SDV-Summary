RoboRobin help

Hello! Thank you for using RoboRobin by upload.farm. This is a tool designed to allow PC Stardew Valley players to add extra cabins to their farm. This isn't officially supported by Stardew Valley and may cause unintended problems! Proceed at your own risk!



Usage instructions

  1. Press "Open Save", and choose your savegame for RoboRobin to work on. This normally looks something like "FarmerName_123456789". This will automatically create a backup of the savegame with a name like "FarmerName_123456789_backup-1525647006.1927967". You can rename this to the original filename to restore your unaltered savegame.
  2. If the savegame is from Stardew Valley version 1.3, it will load into RoboRobin. If the savegame appears older than version 1.3, it will throw up a warning you should pay attention to!
  3. Add new cabins as you like, or remove cabins, using the sliders to decide where the cabins are positioned. RoboRobin is a bit less smart than regular Robin, and will happily build Cabins on top of trees, rocks, water... anywhere.
    Also, be warned, if you use RoboRobin to deconstruct someone's cabin she'll remove any players who were living in there too. RoboRobin takes no prisoners.
  4. Press "Write Save" when you're finished. This will let you play your altered savegame in Stardew Valley.
